Jenny manifesto

From the moment I set foot in the workroom, I will try to do my best in every situation given. Even the days where I am not feeling like the best version of myself, I will find strength and motivation to bring myself up and do a fantastic job. I will remember to think about everyone and everything around me and treat them right, and I will do nice things to others without expecting to receive something back. I will also not forget myself. Amidst all the hard work and busy days, I will take some time to treat also myself. It is extremely important that I listen to my body and let it rest, cause if my body and brain is stressed and tired, I will not be able to work good and hard or think about others.
