ViroBin

ViroBin is a trash can designed to draw attention and encourage people to throw their trash. When someone takes it to use, a message in the form of a fortune or joke will appear on an LED-screen. We hope that this type of reward will make people feel like they get something back for their effort. Every feature on the ViroBin is powered by a solar panel to prevent unnecessary power consumption.

Why we made it

Every day, an estimate of two tons of litter is being thrown on the streets in Oslo. There is a lack of the right attitude towards trash in the city, which results in littering at such extent. We have made the ViroBin with a goal of making people want to throw their trash because of the many features it provides.

Gogreen

Gogreen is an app with a goal of spreading awareness about climate issues and helping people develop habits that have a positive impact on the climate. The users get challenges to complete that encourage to make sustainable choices. For every completed challenge, the users gain points. These points can be traded for discounts and gifts from our partners.
